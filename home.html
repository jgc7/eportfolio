Technology Vision Statement:

Technology is an important source of information and communication linking school, home, community, and the world. Schools should be an environment where all students and staff have equitable access to a full range of current technology, software tools, and applications. The staff should take part in continuous technology professional development to enable teachers to use technology as a tool in their classroom environments. The students should become comfortable using technology, engaging in hands-on learning, stimulating their curiosity and developing their critical thinking skills. As a result, the use of technology enhances the educational process.
